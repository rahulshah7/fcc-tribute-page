Principles by Ray Dalio

In 2011 Ray Dalio self published a 106 page book titled Principles. The book details his fundamental life principles, which are the base of, and extend to form his work principles.

Dalio believes having principles are essential for getting what you want out of life. He defines principles as what connect your values i.e. what you value in life, to your actions. They can be applied again and again in similar situations to help you achieve your goals. Our most valuable principles come from our own experiences and our reflections on those experiences.

Dalio's own principles are described in detail in his original 2011 self published book, available at archive.org as well as summarized in an excerpt to his 2017 book, available at Bridgewater's website.

The 2017 expanded and updated version of the book, titled Principles: Life & Work is available for purchase from amazon. In 2018, an animated mini-series titled Principles For Success was released distilling the lessons regarding Dalio's life principles in to a 30 minute YouTube video.
